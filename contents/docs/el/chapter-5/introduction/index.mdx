---
title: Practical Radiation Protection in Medical Applications
description: Overview of practical radiation protection measures in clinical settings for non-radiation healthcare workers.
---

This module presents the main medical applications of ionizing radiation and outlines the practical measures required to minimize radiation exposure in clinical settings. It focuses on situations that may be encountered by non-radiation healthcare workers and provides guidance on safe behavior in environments where radiation is used. Emphasis is placed on applying fundamental protection principles, such as time, distance, and shielding, in everyday practice, as well as on recognizing potential radiation hazards and responding appropriately.
