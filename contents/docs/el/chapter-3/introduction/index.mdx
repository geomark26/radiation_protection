---
title: Radiobiology and Biological Effects of Ionizing Radiation
description: Overview of how ionizing radiation interacts with biological systems at molecular, cellular, and organism levels.
---

Radiobiology is the scientific field that studies the effects of ionizing radiation on biological systems at the molecular, cellular, tissue, and organism levels. It examines how radiation interacts with biological matter, how cells respond to and repair damage, and the resulting biological consequences. Radiobiology integrates principles of radiation physics with cellular and molecular biology.
